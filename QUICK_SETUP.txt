STEP 5: Update script.js File
================================================================================

Open script.js and find the EMAILJS_CONFIG section (near the top).

Replace these values:

PUBLIC_KEY: 'YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY'
  → Replace with your Public Key from Step 4

SERVICE_ID: 'YOUR_SERVICE_ID'
  → Replace with your Service ID from Step 2

CONTACT_TEMPLATE_ID: 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID'
  → Replace with Template 1 ID from Step 3

SMS_TEMPLATE_ID: 'YOUR_SMS_TEMPLATE_ID'
  → Replace with Template 2 ID from Step 3

NEWSLETTER_TEMPLATE_ID: 'YOUR_NEWSLETTER_TEMPLATE_ID'
  → Replace with Template 3 ID from Step 3

SMS_GATEWAY: '@sms.gateway.com'
  → Update based on your phone carrier:
     - MTN Rwanda: '@sms.mtn.co.rw'
     - Airtel Rwanda: '@sms.airtel.co.rw'
     - Or check with your carrier for email-to-SMS gateway

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Emails not arriving
Solution: 
- Check spam folder
- Verify all IDs are correct in script.js
- Check EmailJS dashboard for delivery logs

Problem: SMS not working
Solution:
- Verify SMS gateway format is correct for your carrier
- Some carriers may not support email-to-SMS
- Consider using a paid SMS service like Twilio

Problem: Form shows "not configured" message
Solution:
- Make sure you replaced ALL placeholders in EMAILJS_CONFIG
- Check that EmailJS library is loaded (check browser console)
